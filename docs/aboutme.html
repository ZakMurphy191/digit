About Me

I am Country boy that is the baby of the family from Pittsburgh Pennsylvania.I have a family of five one brother named Tyler, my mom and dad, as well as my dog named gunner. Gunner is a weimaraner who loves to go on walks. My main hobbies i like to participate in are Dirt biking, hunting, photography, and family time.

I also like to help out my community and participate in cleaning roads, getting food to the poor, and all of the above to make sure everyone else is safe. If I were to describe myself with a quote I believe my personality is best represented by the saying "Be strong but not rude, be kind but not weak, be humble but not timid, be proud but not arrogant."

Out of all of my hobbies i find the most interest in dirt biking, as i am a Monster Energy Kawasaki professional rider. I am honored to be where i am in the racing world, but i do think that all my hard work and time paid off in it. I get to travel the world and race dirt bikes while being paid. So i would like to give you some advice, if you really want to do something, do it and do not look back!
