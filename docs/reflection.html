Without having a system like Wordpress and it shows you right away the design and layout this was definately the hardest step to get started. On the Other hand, Wordpress is extremely hard to navigate, finding myself lost in the site all the time, i believe wordpress is easier for a beguinner but getting over the hump to understand where and how to edit everything is a very big struggle.
