For the Game Analysis assignment I had the option of chosing between many indie games to provide feedback of my thoughts on the game I chose. This was one of my favorite projects that I have completed so far in the Digital Arts and Media major. This Project consisted of analyzing the developers concepts and ideas that the developer's have created over the course of the game and its making. So what game did I choose?

Tadpole Tales

Developer: Andrei Cretu, Giacomo Autili, Tomer Barnea

Tadpole Tales is an indie game that is solely based on to clean up the river from the monsters. I believe that the Monsters represent waste in today's society that is thrown into rivers, and the Tadpoles are the creatures that live amongst that biological environment that wanna live in a clean envirorment.Within the game the Tadpoles have the mission to clean up the river, by shooting them with their mouths using a disinfectant that "cleans them up" which gets them closer to living in a clean environment. The Game Developers really put a lot of time into this game as it has a real life meaning, and I believe that they are trying to spread awareness about our ecosystems. What I mean by this is, they want people to understand that our garbage and polution we put into their "homes" is damaging to them and the other creatures that live there, while also we should be keeping every aspect of the world as clean as possible.

Graphics

The graphics of the game are not very complex, but they are unique in their own way everything within the game was hand drawn which adds flavor to the game. The game gives you the details of the storyline while loading in which added anticipation to help defend the river from the monsters that have entered the river, while the tadpoles want the river to be clean and friendly, so we must help defend and “clean” up the river.

Settings

The settings in tadpole tails are very easy to control you have three settings start extras in options, options allow you to change the graphics within the game start is just in the menu which allows you to play the game and extras show you the back story to the game and has links to all the creators. In all I would say that the game is very easy to maneuver in and is a very pleasant experience.

Characters

Within the game you cannot pick different characters you have to work your way up by getting hearts they give you more health which gives you better characters to defeat the monsters. I feel that this is something the game developer can improve within as people like to make their character into their own. This would give the game a more diverse feeling and have users more derived to play the game.

Within Tadpole Tales you do not get to know the characters that well in a humanly matter, but you do become aware of their abilities and strengths as compared to the characters before. as you move up in hearts you can get bigger and better characters but if you get touched once by any of the monsters you lose a heart and when you have no hearts left the game is over.

Challenges

As a player in the game, I believe it is very hard to get past some levels because you don't start with enough health to deal with all the possible things like can hurt you within the game. Maybe they should add rounds to the game, so you are ready for the next steps instead of just piling them all into one big round.
